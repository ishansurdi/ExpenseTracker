Role views

Each screen knows who it is for.

Instead of one overloaded dashboard, the homepage explains the product through the three daily perspectives that matter most.

Employee

Submit
  • Upload receipt and auto-fill draft expense.
  • Track approval status without asking finance for updates.
  • Review approved and rejected expense history in one place.

Manager

Review
  • See pending approvals with employee, category, and converted value.
  • Approve or reject with comments and move the workflow forward.
  • Understand whether the manager step is required or conditional.

Admin

Control
  • Create employees and managers and define reporting lines.
  • Configure sequential, percentage, or specific-approver rules.
  • See all expenses and intervene when exceptions happen.
